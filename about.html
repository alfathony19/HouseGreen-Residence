About Us

HouseGreen Residence

HouseGreen Residence was born from a vision to create a modern residence that is in harmony with nature. Established in 2024, this housing was developed as an answer to the community's need for a comfortable, environmentally friendly, and sustainable place to live. By combining contemporary architectural design and the concept of green living, HouseGreen Residence emphasizes harmony between humans and the environment.

Slogan:
"Comfortable Housing, Green Future"

Vision:
To become a leading housing complex that presents sustainable modern living, where comfort, beauty, and environmental sustainability go hand in hand to create a better future.

Mission:

  • Developing housing with innovative designs that support a modern and environmentally friendly lifestyle.
  • Prioritizing the use of high-quality and environmentally friendly materials in every development.
  • Building a harmonious and inclusive community that supports the welfare of residents.
  • Providing complete facilities to support the needs of life, education, and family recreation.
  • Actively contributing to maintaining the balance of the ecosystem through greening and responsible waste management.

Owner's Autobiography

The owner of the company, Mr. Aldo Abdillah Fathony, is a visionary entrepreneur who has built the company from scratch. With a background in engineering and business management, he is highly dedicated to innovation.

Thanks to his leadership, the company has won numerous awards and become one of the pioneers in its industry. He believes that success is not only measured by profit but also by positive impacts on society.
