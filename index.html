HouseGreen Residence prioritizes the safety of its residents. Therefore, we provide a sophisticated security system that operates 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. This system includes CCTV monitoring throughout the complex, ensuring that every corner and area is well-guarded. With this, every activity that occurs around the property can be monitored directly, providing a high level of security and providing peace of mind to all residents.
